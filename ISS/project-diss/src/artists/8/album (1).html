"Godfather" is the eleventh studio album by the British rapper and producer, Wiley. The album was released on January 13, 2017, and it features collaborations with other UK grime and hip-hop artists such as Skepta, JME, Ghetts, Devlin, and Chip, among others. The album showcases Wiley's signature sound, which blends grime, hip-hop, and electronic music. It features hard-hitting beats, fast-paced flows, and catchy hooks.

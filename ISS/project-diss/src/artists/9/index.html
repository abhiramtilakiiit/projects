Pete and Bas
In a land not too dissimilar from our own, two elderlies found themselves the unwilling victims of a curse inflicted upon them by a vengeful wizard. Rather than accepting their fate and withering away, the pair instead began to age in reverse, growing stronger and more agile with each passing day. And so, armed with the knowledge and experience of their long lives, the two set out on a quest to clean dem streeetz, and blended in with the Brixton drill scene. Despite the incredulous looks and dismissive comments of those around them, the determined duo pressed on, taking on the toughest challenges and facing down the most fearsome foes with all the courage and cunning they could muster. And as they worked to cleanse the city of its blight, they slowly but surely began to see the world around them with fresh eyes, grateful for the chance to live a life anew.
Now all they have to offer is fine tuned melodies they got inspired during their quest.

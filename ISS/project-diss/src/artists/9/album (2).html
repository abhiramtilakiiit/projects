"The Old Estate" is a song by British rap duo Pete and Bas, released in 2019. The track tells the story of the duo's experiences growing up in their council estate in London, and highlights the struggles and hardships faced by working-class families in the UK. The song features Pete and Bas' signature witty and comedic lyrics, delivered over a stripped-back, bass-heavy beat. The track quickly gained popularity on social media and helped to launch Pete and Bas' career in the UK rap scene.

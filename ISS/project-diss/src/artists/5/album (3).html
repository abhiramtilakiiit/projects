With its eclectic blend of hip-hop, R&B, and pop, the album offers a fresh and contemporary take on this timeless masterpiece. The tracks on the album feature introspective lyrics that reflect on the beauty, complexity, and ambiguity of the Mona Lisa, and the impact it has had on art and culture.

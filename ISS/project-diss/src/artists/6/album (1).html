"Jesus Is King" is the ninth studio album by American rapper and producer Kanye West, released on October 25, 2019. The album features 11 tracks, and many of the songs prominently feature gospel choirs and Christian themes, with lyrics about salvation, redemption, and devotion to God. In terms of production, "Jesus Is King" features a minimalist and stripped-down sound, with West favoring sparse, gospel-inspired beats and instrumentation over his previous maximalist style.

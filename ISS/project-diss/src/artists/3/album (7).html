E3 AF is the seventh studio album by British rapper Dizzee Rascal, released in 2020. The title stands for "E3 African Freestyle" and pays homage to the East London area where Dizzee grew up. The album blends elements of grime, dancehall, and Afrobeats, with features from artists such as Kano, Ghetts, and Afronaut Zu.

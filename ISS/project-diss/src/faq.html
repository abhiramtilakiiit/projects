Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do you choose and rate the music you feature?

We use a rating system that takes into account factors such as lyrics, melody, production, and overall impact. Our ratings range from 1 to 10, with 10 being the highest possible score.

2. Can you recommend similar artists or genres based on the music you feature?

Yes, we often provide recommendations for similar artists or genres based on the music we review. We believe that discovering new music is an important part of the review process.

3. Do you accept sponsored music features?

No, we do not accept sponsored music features. All of our reviews are based solely on the quality of the music and personal opinions.

4. Can I use your music features/reviews on my website or social media?

Yes, you are welcome to share our music features/reviews on your website or social media as long as you credit us and link back to our page.

5. Do you only review new music or can you review older music as well?

We review both new and older music. We believe that good music stands the test of time, so we're always open to reviewing classics as well as new releases.

6. How can I get in touch with you if I have a question or comment?

You can contact us by sending an email to our team. We always strive to respond to inquiries in a timely manner.

7. Can I request a specific album or artist for you to review?

Yes, we welcome requests for specific albums or artists to review. While we can't guarantee that we will review everything that is requested, we do consider all requests and try to accommodate them whenever possible.

8. What types of music do you feature on your page?

We feature a wide variety of genres including rock, pop, hip hop, electronic, and more. We're open to exploring all kinds of music as long as it's good!

9. Can I submit my own music for review?

Yes, we welcome submissions from independent artists and bands. Feel free to get in touch via email.
